### 🎯 Project Objective
Analyzed 1,000 books from [books.toscrape.com](http://books.toscrape.com) to optimize **pricing strategy**, understand **customer preferences**, and improve **inventory management**.

### ✨ Key Features

- **Web Scraping**: Scraped 1000 books across 50 pages with `requests` + `BeautifulSoup`
- **Data Cleaning & Feature Engineering**
- **Exploratory Data Analysis** with interactive visualizations
- **Machine Learning**:
  - K-Means Clustering (3 customer segments)
  - Random Forest Classifier (~73% accuracy)
- **Interactive Power BI Dashboard** with slicers, drill-through, bookmarks & RLS

---

### 📊 Business Insights

| KPI                    | Value          | Insight |
|------------------------|----------------|--------|
| Total Books            | 1,000          | Complete catalogue analysed |
| Avg Price              | £35.07         | Healthy mid-range pricing |
| Avg Rating             | 3.34           | Moderate satisfaction |
| High-Rated Books (4+)  | 42%            | Strong premium segment |
| In-Stock Rate          | 100%           | Excellent inventory |

**Top Recommendation**: Increase price of 4 & 5-star books by 15-20%.
